Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of electric vehicle (EV) battery charge levels and driving range through interactive data visualizations. The objective is to evaluate key performance indicators such as battery capacity utilization, charging duration, energy efficiency, and range distribution across different EV models. The dashboard enables users to gain data-driven insights into charging behavior and vehicle performance, supporting better understanding of efficiency patterns and operational variability. By integrating analytical metrics with dynamic visual tools, the system provides a structured approach to assessing electric vehicle performance and sustainability factors.

Charge and Range Analysis

The visualization provides a comparative analysis of electric vehicles based on key performance metrics, including battery capacity, charging duration, and driving range. By examining these parameters, the system identifies performance variations across different EV models and highlights efficiency trends. This analytical approach supports a clearer understanding of how battery specifications and charging characteristics influence overall vehicle range and operational performance.
